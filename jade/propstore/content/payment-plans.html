Payment Plans

Can’t quite afford that dream prop or costume you’ve always wanted? We’ll make it easier on you. Just use Propstore’s interest-free payment plan.

Choose a plan at checkout
We offer 1, 2, 3 and 4 month plans and if you spend over £x.00 we offer up to 10 month plans
Make a down payment
Make a 20% down payment (non-refundable) on your credit card. After that, you’re free to settle over the next 2 - 6 months.
Get your dream item
When you’ve made all your payments, your dream acquisition will be shipped right to your door.
