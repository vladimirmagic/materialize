    The Reserve Price is the minimum price the Seller will accept for a Lot. The Reserve Price will not be more than the lowest estimate given by Propstore. Please increase your bid to ensure it is above the reserve.

    Bids placed now are max bids which will be executed on your behalf once the auction starts. These bids will be executed at the lowest possible price during the auction, as dictated by other bidding activity. Please follow the auction closely as most bidding activity occurs while the lot is closing - should you be outbid, you will be able to increase your bid at any point until the lot is sold.
