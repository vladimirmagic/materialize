Step 1
Request valuation
Request a free no obligation appraisal and valuation via our quick-form located here. While minimal info is required, the more you can tell us now, the more quickly we can provide an evaluation – you can even request a live video chat to speed the process! Within 72 business hours, we will respond with options for helping you sell your item.
View step 2
Step 2
Consignor’s Agreement
During our appraisal, we will suggest a provisional estimate for your item and may ask you to send the content to us for an in-person final assessment. Once your content is in-house, we will verify its authenticity and make recommendations for selling the item.
Once we have agreed on a sales platform (fixed-price or auction), reserve price (the minimum price at which we will sell your item), and estimate, we will send a Consignor’s Agreement, that confirms our services, outlines fees and covers our terms and conditions of sale.
View step 3
Step 3
Catalog your item
Until it is ready to sell, we will keep your item safe and secure in our high security facility. As we approach our next auction, our team will catalog your item, professionally photograph it and create a compelling entry for it to be sold.
View step 4
Step 4
Sales report
If we help you sell your item via our auction platform, it will be offered to a worldwide market in one of our biannual Entertainment Memorabilia Live Auctions (EMLA) and sold to the highest bidder.
If we help you sell your item via our Buy-Now platform, it will be offered at a fixed price on our website, where visitors to our site can purchase it immediately.
Once your item sells through either Auction or Buy-Now, we will provide you with a comprehensive sales report confirming your proceeds from the sale.
