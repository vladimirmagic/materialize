Stephen

The founder of Propstore, Stephen became interested in movie memorabilia while his previous sign-making company was working as a contractor on the sets of Eyes Wide Shut.

Stephen began networking with Industry technicians and found his first few treasures, which led him to pursue Prop Store as a full time business. At that time, no one in the UK was selling this type of memorabilia as a full-time operation.

Stephen has been a steady pioneer in the industry, constantly developing and evolving the business and constantly trying fresh initiatives. He has located some of the most significant caches of film memorabilia of the past decade and both his personal and company collections reflect this- they are amongst the finest in the world.

Brandon

Brandon worked as a part-time agent for Prop Store throughout 2006, before being brought on to head the US operation in May 2007.

Brandon has a background in researching the production of the Star Wars and Indiana Jones films and had established contact and transacted with many key crew members from these productions before joining the Prop Store team. Brandon is educated within film studies to post graduate level and had published numerous articles on film production and movie memorabilia.

Over the past two years he has worked to develop the Propstore's LA branch to the level of quality that the London office has always been renowned for.
