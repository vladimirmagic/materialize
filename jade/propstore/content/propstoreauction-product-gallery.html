  • Description

    Myth: Does an eyepatch help someone see in the dark? Result: Plausible.

    Scene Reference: Popup Pirate Grant and a Parrot accessory was created for Episode "Pirate Special" (2007, E71), which tested various pirate myths including does an eyepatch help someone see in the dark and can pirates use rum to clean clothing. This was a 2-length special where most of the myths were busted.

    Physical Description: The popup Pirate Grant is made of wood, metal, and paper, and mounted on a wooden stand. The Parrot accessory is composed of plastic, rubber, paint, and feathers. Additionally, a miniature rubber traffic cone completes the set. Marked 5.1-BP-P44A.

    Condition: Due to the nature of the show and the frequent use of the items, all lots will show damage from production including but not limited to broken pieces, damaged surfaces and internal parts, and torn pages. Please review the full photograph sets to fully understand the condition of the lot.

    Dimensions: 29" x 22" x 13" (73.7 cm x 55.9 cm x 33 cm)

      Item Location

    This is an original asset used in the production of MythBusters (TV). The asset is made available by Prop Store in association with the Grant Imahara STEAM Foundation.
